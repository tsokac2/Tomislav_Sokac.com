Bio

With 8 years full-time theater experience, playing in The Lion King show like a Simba, or flirting with a Belle like an evil Gaston in The Beauty and the Beast, from driving a greased lightning with his boys playing Dany Zukko in Grease. To finishing like a Sky walking the sunny beach with Sophie and planning trip around the world.
Tomislav decided that is a time for the next step...


Not long ago he moved from Croatia to Ireland, and successfully finished acting school for TV and Film, with a great teacher and mentor Gillian Reynolds. Shortly after, he shot his personal short film ''The Loan''. Playing a Russian bank robber and gangster ready to kill. Viking 5 production offered Tomislav to play a Viking Chieftain for TV show ''Vikings'' Season 5.


Everything started when Tomislav was still in his college days, where he found out that he is a great dancer and performer. The talent was hidden but when he's salsa, bachata, and disco fox teacher recognized his secret passion all was changed in a day.


You can find Tomislav in a heart of Dublin, Ireland, filming short films with his friend's and sharpening his already amazing talent, day by day.
